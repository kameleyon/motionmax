Your most recent payment didn't go through — the card issuer declined it. This is almost always a transient bank-side check (expired card, new chip rotation, an unfamiliar merchant flag), not anything wrong with your motionmax account.

What happens next:

  • Your account stays fully active during a 7-day grace period. Nothing changes today.
  • We'll automatically retry the charge in 3 days, then again in 5. Most declines clear on the second attempt.
  • If you update your card now via the button below, the next retry uses the new card and the grace period ends cleanly.

If you want to keep the same card, contact your bank and let them know the charge was legitimate — that usually unblocks it within the hour. Stuck? Reply to this email or write to support@motionmax.io.
